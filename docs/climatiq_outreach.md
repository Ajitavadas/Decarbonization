# Climatiq Partnership Outreach

Everything you need to reach out to Climatiq's GTM / partnerships team, with ready-to-send drafts and the
exact steps.

---

## Where to reach out (in priority order)

1. **Primary — Partner program form (verified channel):**
   [https://www.climatiq.io/partner-with-climatiq](https://www.climatiq.io/partner-with-climatiq) →
   *"Connect with a Climatiq Expert"*. This form routes straight to their partnerships team. This is the
   channel their €10M Series A explicitly earmarked ("growing its partner ecosystem"), so it's the
   warmest door.

2. **Secondary — LinkedIn.** Find Climatiq's Head of Partnerships / BD or a GTM lead on the
   [Climatiq company page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/climatiq) and send a short connection note
   (draft below). Founders are Hessam Lavi (Product) and Josef Ferm — a concise founder-to-founder note
   can also land.

3. **Tertiary — General contact.** Use the contact form on
   [climatiq.io](https://www.climatiq.io/) if the partner form stalls. (Avoid guessing a direct email
   address — use their official forms so it reaches the right inbox.)

---

## Exact steps

1. **Publish a live demo** the platform is reachable from (or record a 3-min screen capture — see
   `demo_script.md`). Have the URL or video link ready to paste.
2. **Confirm your commercial intent.** Climatiq's free tier is **non-commercial only**; a commercial
   product needs a commercial data license (Enterprise / Data Pro). So the partnership conversation *is*
   the licensing conversation — go in ready to discuss both.
3. **Submit the partner form** with the short message below. Attach or link the one-pager
   (`one_pager.md`) and the demo.
4. **Same day, send the LinkedIn note** to their partnerships lead referencing the form submission (so
   two touchpoints land together).
5. **Prepare answers to the two questions they will ask:**
   - *Who's your target customer?* → Sustainability/ESG analysts at MNCs under CSRD / supplier-disclosure
     / regional mandates.
   - *What's your differentiation?* → AI-native ingestion (messy data → auto scope-classified, factor-
     backed inventory) + a natural-language Carbon Copilot + automated anomaly auditing — the AI workflow
     layer on *their* data, i.e. exactly the ISV role their partner program monetizes.
6. **Ask explicitly for:** (a) commercial data-licensing terms and volume pricing, (b) co-marketing /
   partner-directory listing, (c) technical enablement contact.
7. **Follow up** after 5–7 business days if no reply, with the demo link and one new proof point.

## Positioning notes (keep in mind on the call)

- **You are not their competitor.** Climatiq sells the data/calculation layer; you're an application on
  top — the exact ISV they monetize. They already partner with app-layer companies that compete with each
  other (Salesforce, Pulsora, Novata, Greenplaces).
- **Salesforce Net Zero Cloud validates the category, not a threat.** Climatiq is a *data partner* of
  Salesforce. Your wedge is the mid-market/analyst segment that enterprise suites price out.
- **Lead with the workflow, not the data.** Their data is the moat you're building on; your value is the
  AI ingestion + copilot + auditing experience.
